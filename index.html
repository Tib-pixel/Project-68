Campus Life

The school is 3 floors and includes a basement. People are really nice and food is good. Staff take care of students that break the rules. You can take the bus to and fro the school. Curriculum is great in developing students. There is an after school program and the are clubs included in the after school. The school hosts a spelling bee and geography bee every year and students have gone to state and beyond.

Achievements

The school has won multiple awards. The school clubs also compete in comptitions. Chess club has won several awards. Math club also has a lot of awards. The school als ohosts the B-Show which is a performance held about the end of the year. Any student that is accepted from the auditions can preform. There are 3 B-Show performances held each year. 2 for the school, and 1 for the parents. There could be more. Due to Covid-19, there was a virtual performance. The trophy case is near the Main Office and the Auditorium.

What's New

Beaubien just made a cookbook and people who are enrolled to the school can enter a recipe. Many other schools have done this. The yearbook is coming up and you can order one right now. There is usually not many changes besides the teachers.
